Zelos
Dark Ritual: Creates an area damage zone that lasts 6s and is 3 tiles wide. Enemies standing inside the zone take 30/40/50% of ATK as damage every 0.5s, and have their movement speed and attack speed reduced by 25/50/75%.
Cooldown reduction: Each unique enemy affected by the skill reduces the skill’s cooldown by 1s (counted once per enemy, not per damage tick).
Cooldown: 32s

Arryn
Arryn fires an ice arrow that deals AoE damage in a 2 / 2.5 / 3-tile radius. Damage is 125% / 175% / 225% of ATK. Enemies hit are frozen for 3s (bosses: 0.3s).
Cooldown: 20s

Miria: 
Enters a channeling state (casts the skill and does not perform basic attacks). She fires magma pellets (small fire rocks with a trail) toward all enemies within her attack range, launching a total of 3 pellets over 1 second. Each pellet deals 50% / 75% / 100% of ATK as damage.
Cooldown: 15s

Lyna:
Lyna cast a spell that increase all allies hero/ tower/ unit 15/25/35% critical chance and 30/50/70% critical damage for 5 second. 
Cooldown: 25s

Wukong:
Wukong: Creates 2 / 3 / 4 clones of himself. Each clone has basic stats equal to 50% / 70% / 90% of Wukong’s stats. Clones last 10s and cannot be attacked. 
Cooldown: 25s.
Spawn behavior:
Clones spawn within the tower’s attack area.
They spawn on tiles that enemies are moving through (enemy path tiles).
Clones use ranged basic attacks.
Each tile can spawn a maximum of 2 clones.
